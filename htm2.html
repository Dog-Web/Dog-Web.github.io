Barn Owl

The barn owl (Tyto alba) is the mostwidely distributed species of owl and one of the most widespread of all birds.

  • Scientific Name: owl.
  • Average Lenght: 32 - 40 cm
  • Average Life's Span: 4 years
  • Habitat: Voles

The barn owl is found almost everywhere in the world except polar and desert regions, Asia north of the Himalayas, most of Indonesia, and some Pacific islands.
